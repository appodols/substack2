A few readers have asked me When do the reflections and analysis about class and American culture come? The answer: The final 4 chapters, and, especially, the final chapter, which ties everything together. In that concluding chapter, I share my thoughts as I stand now, analyzing everything that Id experienced and what it all means. The first half of the book, though, is told from my perspective as a child and as a teenager living in instability, squalor, and dysfunction. It was important for me to communicate those experiences before I offer my more mature observations and thoughts as a writer and psychologist.
