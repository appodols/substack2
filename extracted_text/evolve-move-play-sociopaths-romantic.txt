I've come across Nathan's rough idea in the article on Right's Stupidity a couple times this week. I really want to end wokeness and DEi as well, but arguing that races have different IQs is NOT the way to defeat wokeness. What I learned in community college psychology twenty years ago still holds: there are more differences within groups than between groups. I'm ok that the average man has a higher IQ than the average woman, and that the average asian has a higher iq than the average white person. People should be judged on their own merit, and not be defined by traits they can't control. Over the holidays we watched a lot of 10-20 year old movies, and breaking out of the mold was a recurring motif in the culture and is universally appealing. That idea that people can pull themselves up is one the pendulum needs to swing back to. The argument that some groups have a higher IQ than others--that might be true, but it takes so MUCH more than IQ to win a culture war.
