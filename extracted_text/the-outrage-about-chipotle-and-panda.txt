Its one thing to see Ludacris living a glamorous lifethat feels understandable and unattainablebut seeing someone so similar to yourself succeeding in a way that looks effortless can feel deeply unfair, especially if youre struggling in your own career.
This is becausesimilarity is one of the strongest predictorsof whether you envy someone. The people you envy are those that you could imagine yourself being. The key components are similarity, and achievement in a domain that is relevant to yourself. So if you want to be an influencer, and you see someone of roughly your same age, sex, education level, attractiveness, and so on achieve the ability to earn a living online, you will feel more envy than if it is someone very different from yourself, someone who comes from a completely different background than you.
