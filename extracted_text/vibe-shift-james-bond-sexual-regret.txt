Scott Alexander of ACX has done an excellent takedown of the idea that the brain is immature before age 25.  His essential point is that we must ask the question "What activity are we looking at?"  The 16 y/o brain does wonderfully for some things and does not do better at age 25. Being less impulsive works great for some activities, especially related to impressing older people, but may even be a negative in impressing 18 year olds. As in...reproduction, generally considered important. Or warfare. Yeah, nothing better than an army of 40 year olds when the invasion is at the border.
