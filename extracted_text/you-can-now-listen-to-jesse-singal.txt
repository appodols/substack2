Note that about one hour and 18 minutes into the AEI event I asked Rob whether conscientiousness is innate or can be taught, he replied that it responds to incentives.  This ties into his concept of luxury beliefs.  In a society where the professed beliefs favor conscientiousness people have an incentive to behave that way.  If people who behave that way nonetheless profess contrary beliefs, that will reduce the incentives of others to be conscientious. That will harm people who are less conscientious to begin with
